PrivacyPolicy

This Privacy Policy explains how WatchDash handles information. WatchDash is designed as a simple Apple Watch game with local gameplay progress.

Information We Collect

WatchDash does not require an account and does not ask you to provide personal information inside the app.

The app may save gameplay information locally on your device, such as coins, selected skins, and level completion progress.

How Information Is Used

Local gameplay information is used only to make the game work, remember progress, and keep unlocked items available between play sessions.

Data Sharing

WatchDash does not sell personal information. WatchDash does not share gameplay progress with third-party advertisers or data brokers.

Analytics And Tracking

WatchDash does not include third-party advertising tracking. If future versions add analytics or online features, this policy will be updated before those features are released.

Children's Privacy

WatchDash is a general audience arcade game. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Removal

Gameplay progress is stored locally. Removing WatchDash from your Apple Watch may remove saved coins, skins, and level completion progress.

Changes To This Policy

This policy may be updated when WatchDash changes. The latest version will be posted on this page.
